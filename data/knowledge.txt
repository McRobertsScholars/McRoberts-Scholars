McRoberts Scholars – Club Info & Scholarship Support

What We Do:
McRoberts Scholars is a student-run club that helps peers find, apply for, and win scholarships. We support students with application strategies, essay writing, and interview preparation.

How We Help:

Curated scholarship listings updated regularly

Guidance on eligibility and application steps

Templates for resumes and personal statements

Workshops and Q&A sessions (announced on Discord)

Access to application guides and example essays

Getting Started with Scholarships:

Visit our website to browse current scholarships

Review eligibility criteria carefully

Prepare required documents early (transcripts, references, etc.)

Apply before deadlines—don’t wait until the last day

Join workshops or ask for feedback on essays and resumes
